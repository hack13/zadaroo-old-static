Terrain

Please read the instructions under each terrain picture to see how to load it. For more information go HERE

IMPORTANT NOTE:

With my files you should always start out with water to keep the edges smooth. So first thing you should do before loading one of these terrains is type into your console terrain fill 0 and hit enter (note: that is the number zero not the letter “o”). Then follow the instructions below the terrain picture you want to get your terrain loaded right.

 

This image is not what you use it is just here to show you what it will look like after you load it.
 
Click the picture above and it will take you to a grayscale image of this terrain.Right click the grayscale picture on the left and choose “save image as” and save it to the bin folder of your OpenSim.Then in your console for the region you want this loaded on you type in  terrain load terrain-apple.png   and hit enter.
